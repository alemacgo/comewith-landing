About

ComeWith is a social network that connects you with those who are socially close to you through spontaneous activities. It allows you to get together with friends and friends of friends.

By inviting friends and allowing for friends of friends to be invited, you create the right conditions for new spontaneous and serendipitous relationships to form in your network.
